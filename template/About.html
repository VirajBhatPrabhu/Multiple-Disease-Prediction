ABOUT ROBOCARE



AIM BEHIND ROBOCARE ?

Providing accurate and accessible diagnoses is a fundamental challenge for global healthcare systems .In the US alone an estimated 5% of outpatients receive the wrong diagnosis every year. These errors are particularly common when diagnosing patients with serious medical conditions, with an estimated 20% of these patients being misdiagnosed.

In recent years, artificial intelligence and machine learning have emerged as powerful tools for solving complex problems in diverse domains.Machine learning has demonstrated truly life-impacting potential in healthcare – particularly in the area of medical diagnosis



WHAT DO WE DO ?

The potential of machine learning within the medical industry is revealed through this in-depth example of how the technology can be applied to provide a medical diagnosis – in this case, the detection and diagnosis of Brain Stroke,Heart Failure, Breast Cancer, Liver Disease, Prostate Cancer, Diabetes



WHY ROBOCARE ?

Robocare is a web app which has several machine learning models running in the backend. Each disease runs on a certain model trained to predict the possibility of that disease occuring given the patient inputs. 

The models are based on various medical  dataset  which contain thousands of patient records. The codes for this project can be found at my github repo
